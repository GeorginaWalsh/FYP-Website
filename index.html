An Exploration into Data Collection

How Regulated Data Collection should be and how Technological Based Companies use data indirectly influence Consumers


Project Description

Within this research project, the current state of data collection and its regulations will be examined. The objective of this paper is to inform readers enough regarding the topic of data collection, and its regulations, in order to answer two questions:

  • How regulated should consumer data and data collection be?
  • What are some of the possible ways in which the collection and regulation of consumer data may develop?
Rather than trying to enforce a singular answer to these questions, a qualative research approach will be used instead. By making use of content analysis and case study analysis, the reader will be able to form their own opinions and conclusions to the questions above.

Through this paper, an understanding towards how data collection and regulation developed into what it is today should be gained. From knowing its past and current developments, it is possible for anyone to make their own predictions towards what could occur in the future, and decide if they believe it is necessary.
